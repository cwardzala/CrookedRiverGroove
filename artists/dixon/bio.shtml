Don Dixon

Think of pop music - think of Don Dixon. Whether he’s playing, writing or producing, he’s capturing the essence of the moment. As a solo artist, Dixon’s released eight albums, toured all over the US and Europe, and written songs recorded by Joe Cocker, Counting Crows, Hootie and the Blowfish, Marti Jones, and Ronnie Spector, to name a few. His list of credits also includes playing with Mary Chapin Carpenter, Golden Ponies, and more, and producing for Kim Carnes, Hootie and the Blowfish, REM, among others. Now in his fifties, he’s still cranking out tunes that’ll tickle your pop bone!
