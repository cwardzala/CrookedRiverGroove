Uptown Sinclair

  • Dave Hill: Vocals/guitar
  • Tim Panin: Guitar
  • Bill Watterson: Bass
  • Gerry Porter: Drums

Straight from the diamond-paved streets of Cleveland comes Uptown Sinclair, the intercontinental champions of the hot rock action. Uptown Sinclair is the sound of delicious melodies, tantalizing grooves and the umlaut-worthy guitar riffs all melding together. Think of what might happen if Barry White were to front Led Zeppelin, only without all the fancy catering and all those ridiculous dry-cleaning bills, and you're starting to get the picture.
