Tommy Wiggins

  • Tommy Wiggins: Organ, vocals
  • Al Moss: Guitar/backing vocals

Tommy Wiggins writes, sings and plays story songs influenced by folk, rock and blues. In northeast Ohio he’s known as “The Studio Guy”, but in a music career spanning 30 years, he’s performed at countless concerts, taverns and coffeehouses across the country.

On Tommy’s eighth recording, 2001’s Big Apple Hum, he reflects on his life since moving to Ohio in 1995.

“There I go again, working up a passion
Try to pull some feeling from this day to day
Grinding out a living, fightin’ off the latest fashion
Makes you wonder how we got that way.”
