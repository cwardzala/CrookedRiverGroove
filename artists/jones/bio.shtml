Hayshaker Jones

  • Clint Holley: Vocals and acoustic guitar
  • David Bowling: Electric guitar
  • Mark Biviano: Bass and vocals
  • Johnny Jail: Lead guitar
  • Gary Morgan: Drums

Hayshaker Jones - explosive American music — has found a spot in music that is unique. Blending the styles of country and rock music combined with passion and integrity, this band has built a reputation for its live performances over the past year and a half. Sharing stages with Aaron Tippin, David Allen Coe and Hank Williams III as well as playing on The Fox 8 Morning Show and at The Cleveland Rib Burn Off has given the band the confidence and credentials to play all over Northern Ohio.
