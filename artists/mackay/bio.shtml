Cindy MacKay

  • Cindy MacKay : Guitar/lead vocals
  • Colette Gschwind : Vocals

Enchanting qualities lend a dreamlike atmosphere to sing/songwriter Cindy MacKay. Using guitar, dulcimer and mandocello, Cindy performs with a warm finger picking style interlocking the instruments with timeless vocal melodies and harmonies. Cindy?s voice is simply stunning, guaranteed to mesmerize anyone who takes the time to listen

Cindy showcased at the International Alliance Folk Festival 2000. Her CD Patterns in Wave and various Cleveland based music compilations have awarded her favorable reviews from Louisiana to Lithuania.
