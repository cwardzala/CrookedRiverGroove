Alexis Antes

  • Alexis Antes: Vocals/acoustic guitar
  • Tina Drake: Drums
  • Derek Poindexter: Bass
  • Robin Stone: Percussion

Folk/Pop/Rock Singer/Songwriter Alexis Antes is catching the masses up in her grabbing songs about triumph and tribulation. On her debut release, Stronger, she carefully crafts her experiences and personal breakthroughs. Alexis has shared the bill with renowned artists such as Sarah McLachlan, Paula Cole, Sheryl Crow, Melissa Etheridge, Chantal Kreviazuk, Jonatha Brooke and James Taylor and was the only Cleveland artist chosen to perform at the Cleveland Lilith Fair stop in 1998. This Indiegrrl found continued success as a performer at the 1999 Cleveland Scene Music Awards and in Time Warner Cable?s RoadRunner Rocks Battle of the Bands Competition.
