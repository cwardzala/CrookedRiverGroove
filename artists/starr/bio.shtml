Frankie Starr

  • Frankie Starr: Guitar/vocals
  • Lenny Fatigati: Bass
  • August Oswall III: Drums

Blues guitarist Frankie Starr is one of the hardest working musicians in Northeast Ohio. Frankie has played hundreds of gigs every year since he exploded on the scene as a hot-shot teenage guitar-slinger in the late-80’s. Since then, he has matured into a consummate Bluesman whose songs, sometimes venturing from a more traditional blues format, remain “true blue” to the genre. As one of Frankie’s songs tells us, “The Blues is Good for You.” Frankie Starr - (216) 663-1646 jonistarr@prodigy.com
