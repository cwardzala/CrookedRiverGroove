Volume 1 & 2 Artists

Volume 1 & 2 is a double CD compliation that features 25 live performances from our first 25 Crooked River Groove broadcasts. In most cases, the song is the artist’s own choice. With such talented and creative musicians living and working in N.E. Ohio, Recording Arts and Technology and SMART TV will never run out of excellent programs.
