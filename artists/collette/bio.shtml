Colette

  • Colette Gschwind: Guitar/vocals

Colette is an accomplished singer/songwriter/guitarist who uses her rich, powerful voice and an easy-going onstage manner to present a crowd-pleasing combination of friendly humor and musical passion. Her debut CD, Sometimes Patience, is an all-original acoustically flavored project that has received widespread critical acclaim. Taste This, her second CD, is a band-based all-original effort featuring an uptempo alternative pop appeal that cuts across multiple musical formats.
Colette’s widely requested original songs are characterized by sensitive lyrics and a keen sense of the human spirit seen in everyday life - with an emphasis on presenting these emotions in an easily accessible manner. Colette is also an established visual artist who is known for her paintings.
