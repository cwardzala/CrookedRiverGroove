Kevin McCarthy

For the past several years, singer/songwriter Kevin McCarthy has attended college, contemplated a career in art, and moved around the midwest and northeast several times. However, one thing McCarthy has always been is a songwriter, and the years of introspection continually lead him back to the music.

McCarthy's influences are all over the musical map, yet somehow he takes rock, blues, jazz and folk and merges them in a magical way. His voice is smooth, yet has the deep coffee-and-cigarette tones that are the trademark of many singer/songwriters, and his lyrics show wisdom well beyond his age of 22.
