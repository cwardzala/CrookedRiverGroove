Geronimo's Cadillac

In March of 2000, Geronimo’s Cadillac formed in an impromptu jam session at bass player Larry Kalal’s house.  Drawing from myriad musical influences and backgrounds, Geronimo’s Cadillac drives both rock and jazz sides, delivering spirited originals and cover songs done the GC way.

Geronimo’s Cadillac has played and vocalized with a wide range of established, renowned national acts; DJ Logic and Project Logic, Spearhead with Michael Franti, Dark Star Orchestra, Liquid Soul, Strangefolk, Keller Williams, and Michael Ray.

Geronimo’s Cadillac stands ready to spread their groove to anyone that’ll hop in the Cadillac. Get ready for a ride!
