Cletus Black Revue

  • Cletus Black: Vocals/guitar
  • Kevin McCarthy: Guitar
  • Pat Walsh: Bass Guitar
  • Michael Dreyfuss: Electric Viola
  • Dave Morrison: Harmonica

With four independent CD releases to his credit, Cletus Black has developed a reputation as a compelling and prolific songwriter. Each song is a story in itself, all delivered with Cletusx trade mark gritty vocals and gutbucket grooves. Big City Blues magazine called him xan elite wordsmith with a unique musical vision...rockin blues noir!x To follow his vision, Cletus has assembled a formidable live performance unit in the Revue. Cletus and the Revue plan to release their first band CD, Not Too Blues in 2002.
