The music of Anne E. DeChant combines concise and undeniable melodies with thought provoking lyrics in a style that is best described a muscular pop/folk. She is a story-teller who leaves no topic uninspired - one moment the omnipresent spectator, the next a character within the song. Her topics reach from home-town family comforts to the conflicts of war and higher powers. • Anne E. maximizes her song-writing prowess with a striking vocal presence. This theatrical combination affords Anne E. a diverse range of expressions from intimate to gutsy, but always impassioned proclamations.
